# Workflow: Morning Alpha (10 Minutes)

**Trigger:** "Morning alpha" / "Run the morning workflow" / "What happened overnight?"

## What This Does

A structured 10-minute morning ritual that gets you from zero to informed: market context, active narratives, open positions check, and today's best opportunity.

---

## Run in Order — Don't Skip Steps

### 1. Market Pulse (2 min)
Fetch and report:
- BTC price and 24h change
- ETH price and 24h change
- SOL price and 24h change (and any other positions the user holds)
- Total crypto market cap and 24h change
- Fear & Greed Index (current reading)
- Any macro events overnight: Fed statements, major equity moves, geopolitical news affecting risk sentiment

One-line read: **Risk-on / Risk-off / Neutral** — and why.

### 2. Narrative Check (3 min)
Apply the **narrative-radar** skill. Surface:
- What narrative moved overnight?
- Anything newly emerging in the last 8-12 hours?
- Any previously hot narrative that's fading?

One-sentence summary per narrative. No deep dives — flag anything worth researching today.

### 3. Open Positions (2 min)
Apply the **portfolio-risk** skill. Report:
- Each open position: current price vs. entry, unrealized P&L, distance to stop-loss
- Any stop-losses that are close to being triggered?
- Any positions up significantly — should a partial be taken?

### 4. Today's Best Opportunity (3 min)
Based on steps 1-3, surface the single best opportunity to research or act on today:
- Name it
- Why today (what's the catalyst or window)?
- Which skill to use next: token-research, defi-protocol-eval, onchain-intel, or arbitrage-scout?
